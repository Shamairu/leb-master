This year we're going to be in the heart of San Francisco at the SFJAZZ Center.

Travel Grant

We believe a diversity of attributes, experiences, and perspectives are needed to build tools and apps that can change the world. So, we’ve partnered with the Women Techmakers team to offer travel grants to women in technology interested in attending. Apply here to join us in San Francisco.

SFJAZZ Center 201 Franklin Street San Francisco CA 94102

Public Transport

The venue is easily accessible by public transportation, including:

  • BART - Civic Center/UN Plaza station
  • Muni - Civic Center and Van Ness stations
  • Caltrain - San Francisco station

By Car

Attendees who plan to drive can find nearby parking at:

  • Performing Arts Garage - 360 Grove Street
  • Civic Center Garage - 355 McAllister Street
  • California Parking - 110 Franklin Street

By Air

If you're coming from further away, the closest airports are SFO and OAK.

We’re also offering attendees a Lyft discount code for 20% off two rides (maximum discount of $10) to and from the SFJAZZ Center during the event. Look for the code in the next email from us!

  • Hotel Carlton - 1.3 Miles
  • The Fairmont San Francisco - 1.9 Miles
  • St. Regis San Francisco - 2.0 Miles
  • Orchard Hotel - 2.2 Miles

Child Care

We’re excited to offer child care at Chrome Dev Summit 2016! Contact us at chrome-dev-summit@google.com to learn more. Please note that we have limited availability, but we will do our best to accommodate all requests.

Prayer Room and Mother’s Room

We will have a prayer room and Mother’s Room available onsite throughout the event. If you are interested in utilizing these spaces, please ask any registration staff member when you check in to learn more.

Accessibility Accommodations

If you have any accessibility accommodation needs, please let us know by emailing chrome-dev-summit@google.com. We will provide live captioning services in the general sessions.
